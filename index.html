About Me

I'm Alex Singh, a cybersecurity student with a deep curiosity for how digital systems can be compromised — and hardened. My journey started by breaking stuff in my home lab, and it hasn't stopped since.

I actively compete in CTF (Capture The Flag) competitions where I sharpen skills in reverse engineering, binary exploitation, web hacking, and OSINT. Every flag captured is a new lesson in how attackers think.

My goal is to work in penetration testing or security operations, helping organisations find and fix their vulnerabilities before attackers do. I believe in ethical, responsible security research.

Outside the terminal: I document my learning on my blog, contribute to open-source security tools, and help peers in online security communities.
